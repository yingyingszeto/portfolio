Sales Portal IA Redesign

At Manulife Asia, insurance agents used local digital tools to track application statuses. To drive a consistent experience across markets, the Asia team planned to consolidate all the local tools into a core digital tools. While this core design is straightforward as most markets follow a standardized workflow, but there are also differences due to country regulation. I was tasked to work on the UX design of the new feature that was going to launch in four Asian markets—Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines.
