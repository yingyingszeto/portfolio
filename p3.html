AgentSAM

Delivered 3 app features under a tight timeline to boost user adoption

MY ROLE

In 2023, I worked as a UX Designer on AgentSAM, an agent productivity app for Manulife Hong Kong

THE CHALLENGE

To design a mobile experience for three core desktop features within a tight timeline — while ensuring we built the right solution

From day one, we worked with PO, PM, and the Engineering team to align our design process with development

This ensured our work stayed in sync with sprint cycles, project timelines, and evolving priorities

Despite tight timeline, prioritizing early user testing helped us refine the scope and minimize rework during sprint cycles

1st round to get the scope right, 2nd round to validate key flows before development

To buy time for user testing, we worked with engineers to begin foundational components while the design evolved

Core components (buttons, headers, cards) remained consistent, even as higher-level decisions shifted

Grounded in testing insights, the final design gave the product team confidence for the initial launch desipte the tight timeline

Those insights also informed future improvements, with a plan to continue monitoring usage after each feature release

1. Customer Search

Being able to access customer accounts when working outside of office significantly improves agents' workflow

2. Form Search

The ability to send multiple forms to customers with just a click on agents' mobile devices—or print them from anywhere—is a game changer

3. Contests & Incentives

Timely promotion updates serve as a sales motivator, enabling agents to promptly notify customers and maximize upselling opportunities
