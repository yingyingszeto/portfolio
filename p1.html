DTK

Designed a status tracking feature for four different markets

MY ROLE

In 2022, I worked as a UX Designer at Manulife Regional on a productivity app (DTK) that launched in Vietnam, Philippines, Indonesia, and Malaysia

THE CHALLENGE

To help agents track and manage policy application statuses, allowing them to promptly resolve issues with pending applications

A customizable design for all four markets drove consistency and made improvements easier to implement at a regional level

We first designed the core user flow with variations to accommodate country-specific requirements

Introduced a robust notification system that ensures agents never miss critical status updates like pending documents

Clearly defined logic for each status level and created design guidelines for push notification, in-app alerts, and reminders

The final design prioritized on-the-go context, where important updates were easy to spot with more details when explore further

Agents spend 70% of their time working outside the office, they are likely to look at these updates while multi-tasking

After testing with 20 agents across four markets and iterating the design, the content is now easy to scan and provides the right amount of information for each layer, effectively serving different contexts and needs

With an intuitive timeline view that clearly shows agents their next steps
