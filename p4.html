maaiiConnect

Designed an onboard experience

MY ROLE

In 2020, I worked as a UX/UI Designer on maaiiConnect (now rebranded as Cinnox), a customer support platform via live chat on business websites

THE CHALLENGE

To transform new user onboarding by making the LiveChat Widget installation accessible even for those without a technical background

The original onboarding slides failed to properly instruct new users, leading to frequent requests for customer support

Users rarely give products a second chance if they find them difficult to use on the first try

We redesigned it with a 3-step installation onboarding that provides users with a dedicated space to set up the live chat widget

An interactive onboarding is the way to go

Along side with informational graphic instructions that are both intuitive and engaging

Rather than the ambiguous illustrations and blocks of text used originally

After the installation, users are provided with an interactive walkthrough that shows options to personalize the widget further

Just like having an in-person product demo

Users can quickly access the help center through a dedicated in-app window whenever they need support

Getting help without leaving the product
