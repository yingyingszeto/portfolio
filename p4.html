maaiiConnect

Designed an onboard experience

MY ROLE

In 2020, I worked as a UX/UI Designer on maaiiConnect (Rebranded as Cinnox), a LiveChat application

THE CHALLENGE

To guide users on how to install the LiveChat function on their website without the need for customer support assistance

The installation required technical knowledge, the original pre-boarding slides failed to provide effective instructions

After the slides, users was confused how to start navigating the product

An interactive pre-boarding is the way to go

Users understand the product by doing

Along side with straightforward graphic instructions

Onboard users with an interactive walkthrough like a personal sales experience

Getting help without leaving the product

And more... let's talk! And more... let's talk!
