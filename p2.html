ADO

Redesigned the information architecture for an insurance agent portal

MY ROLE

In 2023, I worked as a UX Designer at Manulife Hong Kong to revamp a 20-year-old agent portal (ManuTouch, now ADO)

THE CHALLENGE

To restructure the information architecture to accommodate new layouts that follow the company design system, while ensuring easy adoption by existing agents

Through a content inventory and analysis of usage records from the existing portal, we removed 20% of obsolete content

Working cloely with the operation team, we agreed to only keep functions that served a clear purpose

Reorganized the menu system based on agent task types, following universal workflow patterns that agents naturally understand

Aligning with natural workflows makes the navigation more intuitive and efficient

Conducted tree testing that received over 300 responses, the insights gathered helped secure stakeholder buy-in for the final IA design direction

These insights enabled us to refine the IA's labeling and grouping structure
